The Studio

CUSTOM WORK.
RESIDENT ARTISTS.
REAL CRAFT.

Red Light is a custom tattoo studio located on the Spot on the Square in downtown San Marcos. A dedicated space for working artists and serious craft.

Every piece here begins with a conversation. Whether you're walking in for something small or sitting for a full sleeve, our focus is on precision, respect, and permanence.

Our studio operates with the discipline of a professional environment and the range of a genuine art practice. We keep the doors open late, mirroring the energy of the city.

What We Do

STYLES &
SPECIALTIES

BLACK & GRAY

From subtle fine-line to deep photorealistic shading. Precision, smooth gradients, and controlled contrast designed to hold over time.

VIBRANT COLOR

Saturated, illustrative, and built to stay bright. Neo-traditional, anime, and custom color work applied with solid fundamentals.

FINE LINE

Quiet and precise. Delicate line work with minimal fill — script, botanicals, and geometric forms executed with restraint.

BOLD BLACKWORK

High-impact. Heavy blacks, graphic forms, and negative space handled with intention. Designed to read clearly from across a room.

GEOMETRIC

Structured, architectural, and optically satisfying. Symmetry, mandala work, and sacred geometry lined with mechanical discipline.

CUSTOM FLASH

Walk in, pick from the wall, walk out with a solid piece. Original artist-drawn sheets available on walk-in days — no appointment needed.

Answers

COMMON
QUESTIONS

01

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes. Walk-ins are welcome based on artist availability. Smaller work and flash are most likely to be accommodated same-day.

02

How do I book an appointment?

Book online through our booking portal, call the studio at (737) 335-2261, or direct message us on Instagram @redlightattoostudio. Include your idea, placement, and size.

03

Is a deposit required?

Yes, a deposit is required to secure an appointment. It is applied to the final cost of your session and is non-refundable for no-shows or late cancellations.

04

Can I request a specific artist?

Absolutely. Each artist maintains a distinct style and focus. We recommend reviewing their portfolios before booking to align your concept with the right execution.

05

How should I prepare for my session?

Eat a substantial meal beforehand, hydrate, and wear comfortable clothing that provides easy access to the placement area. Arrive rested and on time.

06

What are your aftercare instructions?

Your artist will provide precise aftercare instructions upon completion of your piece. Follow their guidance strictly, as methods vary based on application technique and skin type.

07

Where is the studio located?

We are located at 109 E. Hopkins St., Suite 206, in the Spot on the Square building in downtown San Marcos. Street parking and nearby lots are available.

08

What are your operating hours?

We run late. For exact daily hours, check our Instagram @redlightattoostudio or call the shop at (737) 335-2261.
